Meet The Chef

"A Jersey Girl with a Southern Flair"

Carolyn Murphy

Chef Carolyn Kyra “Dominique” Murphy started Dominique’s Dishes in 2012 while traveling around the world. She would always take delight in the food wherever she was. Chef Carolyn has been working in the kitchen with her grandparents, parents and other relatives and community.

She loves to ask questions and wonders "why?" With such curiosity, she saw different cultures and they always brought her right back to her own. Whether cooking for community leaders, church events, officiants, basketball teams, weddings, and more, chef Carolyn is always cooking from her soul.

Find me @dominiquesdishes!
